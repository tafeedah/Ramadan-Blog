ABOUT US

WHO ARE THEY?

Ramadan Blog was launched in March 2023 to contribute to presenting authentic mainstream Islam to the world. The aim of Ramadan Blog is to help Muslims around the world grow in faith and spirituality, while at the same time lead productive lives, as Islam teaches, in the context of the modern life of the 21st century. Ramadan Blog also aims to help support new Muslims in learning their religion in a positive way away from extremism.

In this era, when fornication is very common to gain likes and shares. Our website believes in providing authentic and credible news, stories, and blogs to our readers.
